## About Us

We are a two person team that builds custom software. We started as students who worked on the same problem sets. Then we started making side projects together. EliteDevs is the umbrella for everything we ship. 

We make browser extensions, games, productivity tools, and web simulations. Most of our tools started because we wanted to fix our own problems. We build a small version that works, ship it fast, and keep adding features if people use it. We are not a slow agency. We just love to build clean software that works well.

## How We Work

We do not use corporate buzzwords. Here is our real process:
1. **Talk it through:** You tell us your idea. We tell you what parts are easy, what parts are hard, and if it is worth doing. No long PDF proposals.
2. **Ship the ugly version:** We build the smallest working version in week one. It might look bad, but you get to test it and tell us what to fix before we design the rest.
3. **Polish where it matters:** We make the parts you touch every day feel smooth with good animations and performance. We do not waste time on things users will never see.
4. **Hand it off cleanly:** You get the full code, the deployment setup, and a clean readme file. Anyone can read our history and understand the code.
